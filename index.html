Family Holidays!

As a family, we have travelled to many places all around the world: Italy, Fance, America, Germany, Switzerland, Wales and Spain. Holidays with my family are always very special, and I feel privlaged to be able to visit so many amazing places. Last Summer, we went to New York City, for a week; we climbed the Empire State, went to museums and took walks through the iconic 'Central Park.' We then drove - a six hour drive - back down the continent to a State near Boston, where we spent the rest of our Summer holiday, swimming, playing tennis and water skiing!

But two years ago, when Covid said "NO!" to any sort of international travel, my family and I faced the twelve hour drive to Switzerland, during which we stopped three times along the way, at different areas in France. When we finally arrived, for four weeks we enjoyed: Hiking through the tall mountians, swimming in the alpine lakes, and going mount biking! This is was one my most memeroable summer's; it was something that we normally would never had done, and it was a new expirience for everyone, proving to be extremley enjoyable.
